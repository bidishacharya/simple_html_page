We are pre-occupied by this belief that government is never able to meet up to the expectations of the public. We blame government for amost everything. Electricity goes off in your house - BLAME government; the roads you walk is muddy - BLAME government; you fail an exam - BLAME government (just being sarcastic). The point here is: all of us are in this blaming game and no matter what happens, we will be blaming the government.

The purpose of this website is to make your blaming easier and more reasonable. For instance, if you are in a workplace and everybody is shouting and whining about the government, you can actually give them the reasons; the situations where government is missing out to complete the task they've overtook. This will give a strength to your complain.

Also, this website will give a kind of positive pressure to the government. They would not want to see this site go viral and make people hate them logically, right?
